Why VYBE?

VYBE is a colabritive project that was designed with the main focus to enhance the users mood with music.

The music that is chosen is based upon user input of mood, or weather in addition to their prefrences from their spotify account.

Once chosen, a playlist is provided for the users enjoyment.
